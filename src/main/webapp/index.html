TEAMMATES is an online peer feedback and peer evaluation tool for student projects

What is Peer Evaluation?


Peer Evaluation and Feedback is a process whereby students are asked to assess and comment about their teammates work within a group project. It is a very useful tool, that is used widely across different courses in academic instituitions worldwide. The process of Peer Evaluation and Feedback has numerous benefits, both to the students and the course instructors. These benefits include:


  • Helping to encourage higher order reasoning and cognitive skills amongst the students
  • Providing an incentive for students to produce higher quality work
  • Encouraging students to learn from their own mistakes
  • Improving the quality of learning
  • Allowing instructors to identify team management / communication problems easily
  • Allowing instructors to grade students better

Why Use TEAMMATES?


TEAMMATES is an free, online Peer Evaluation Tool for student projects. It provides a content management interface to allow course instructors to easily execute Peer Evaluation assessments within the courses in their academic institution. TEAMMATES currently has over 20,000 submissions within the system, with more being added daily. TEAMMATES provides:


  • A simple and user friendly interface to manage your courses and evaluations for each course
  • The option to have confidential and anonymous feedback and comments
  • Email reminders to the students to inform them when evaluation deadlines are approaching
  • Summarized and detailed reports for each evaluation
  • Publishing the results of each evaluation for students to view
