Features


TEAMMATES is an online Peer Evaluation/Feedback System for use with student team projects. It is free for use, and all you require is to Request for an Account from us.

  • Create new Courses and easily import students teams into the course.
  • Start a new Evaluation for the course and allow students to provide peer feedback to team members and confidential peer evaluations to the instructor.
  • View Reports and Summaries of the evaluation, and moderate them before publishing the results for students to view.
